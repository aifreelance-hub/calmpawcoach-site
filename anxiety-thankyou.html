Your Complete 14-Day Training Program

Day 1 — Safety Check & Baseline
Prepare a calm environment and record your dog’s starting anxiety level.

Day 2 — Micro Absences
Practise 5–10 second absences that end before anxiety rises.

Day 3 — Predictable Leaving Routine
Teach your dog a calm, consistent “owner leaving” routine.

Day 4 — Calm Alone Moments
Build tiny moments of independence within the same room.

Day 5 — Add Mild Sounds
Introduce soft environmental cues during short absences.

Day 6 — Increase Absence Duration
Extend alone time to 30–45 seconds, only if calm.

Day 7 — Build Consistency
Repeat micro absences 5–6 times daily.

Day 8 — First 1–2 Minute Absence
Practice stepping away for 60–90 seconds.

Day 9 — Calm Independence Skills
Encourage resting alone even while you are home.

Day 10 — Moderate Absences
Build up to 2–3 minute absences in multiple repetitions.

Day 11 — Door Practice
Teach your dog that door sounds do not mean panic.

Day 12 — Short Outdoor Absences
Step outside for 30–60 seconds for the first time.

Day 13 — Real-World Duration
Extend outside absences to 2–4 minutes.

Day 14 — Review & Long-Term Plan
Evaluate progress and set weekly maintenance sessions.
