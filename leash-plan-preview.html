10-Day Training Plan

Leash Walking Mastery

A gentle, structured programme to teach your dog to walk calmly by your side without pulling.

Short walks · Clear exercises · Real-life practice

This is a digital training plan included in your CalmPaw Complete Pack. You can follow each day directly on this page.

Day 1 — Equipment & Safe Start Set up

Choose safe, comfortable equipment and introduce it positively.

  • Fit a well-fitting harness or flat collar (no choke/prong).
  • Let your dog wear the gear indoors with treats and praise.
  • Keep the first session very short and positive.
Day 2 — Reward “By My Side” Foundation

Teach your dog that being close to your leg means rewards.

  • Stand still, mark and reward when your dog appears near your left/right leg.
  • Add a cue like “close” once they understand.
  • Keep 3–4 mini sessions, 2–3 minutes each.
Day 10 — Real-World Walk & Next Steps Maintain

Put everything together on a normal walk and decide how you will keep practising.

  • Pick a quiet route and use all skills from previous days.
  • Reward often for loose leash and check-ins.
  • End on success and plan 2–3 “training walks” per week.
