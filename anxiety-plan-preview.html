14-Day Training Plan

Separation Anxiety Reset

A gentle step-by-step schedule to help your dog feel safer and calmer when home alone.

Tiny absences · Calm routines · Gradual confidence building

This is a digital training plan included in your CalmPaw Complete Pack. Follow one small step each day.

Day 1 — Safety Check & Baseline Foundation

Make sure your dog’s environment is safe and comfortable before you start.

  • Choose a quiet, safe area where your dog will stay when alone.
  • Provide water, a comfy bed, and a safe chew.
  • Record how long your dog can stay calm when you step out of sight.
Day 2 — Micro Absences Seconds only

Practise very short absences that end before your dog panics.

  • Step out of the room for 5–10 seconds, then come back calmly.
  • Repeat several times, always returning while your dog is still relatively calm.
  • Do 2–3 short sessions per day.
Day 14 — Review & Long-Term Plan Maintain

Review your dog’s progress and decide how you will keep building confidence.

  • Write down what triggers still cause worry and keep absences shorter than that.
  • Plan 3–4 calm-alone sessions each week going forward.
  • Contact a qualified behaviourist if your dog still cannot cope at all.
