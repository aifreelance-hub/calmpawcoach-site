Refund Policy

We want CalmPaw Coach to be genuinely helpful for you and your dog. Because all products are digital training plans that can be downloaded and copied immediately after purchase, we use the policy below.

Digital products

All CalmPaw Coach programs and packs are delivered digitally. You gain access to the training material right after successful payment.

Change of mind

If you simply change your mind after purchase, we generally do not offer refunds once the material has been accessed or downloaded. Please read the program descriptions carefully before purchasing.

Accidental duplicate purchases

If you accidentally purchase the same program or pack more than once using the same email address, please contact us within 14 days at support@calmpawcoach.com with your order details. We will review and, if confirmed, refund the duplicate charge.

Technical issues

If you experience technical problems accessing your training plans (for example you did not receive your files or links), please contact us and we will work with you to restore access. A refund will only be offered if we are unable to provide access within a reasonable time.

How to request support

To request help or ask about a possible refund, please email:

  • Your full name
  • The email used at checkout
  • Your order ID or payment receipt
  • A short description of the issue

We will review your request in good faith and reply as quickly as we can.

Legal rights

Nothing in this policy limits any rights you may have under applicable consumer law in your country of residence.
